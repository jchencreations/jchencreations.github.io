Late Night Drive in Philly

Medium: Autodesk Maya and Davinci Resolve

Description: This was my final project for my 3D modeling class. The goal was to create a short animation, and I chose to do a sort of stylistic way of showing off a car model and the city of Philadelphia. I designed the car myself based off of various Aston Martin and Mclaren builds. I learned how to import STL files of the city's geography and buildings and convert that into something more efficient for animation. I also reworked Maya's Toon Shader to create a stylized cartoon effect on the car and the environment to create the ramp shading. I then used Davinci Resolve to create the halftones and the speed lines. I also did a bit of audio engineering to create a more realistic sound experience.
