Aoguara

Medium: Adobe Photoshop

Description: My goal here was to design concept art for a cohesive alien planet, where the player is an astronaut trying to do studies on this planet. I was heavily inspired by Subnautica, but instead of horror, I wanted to focus more on scientific studies and exploration. It was very fun designing plants, animals, and vehicles, that fit into my genre of whimsical discovery with more abstract aesthetics.
